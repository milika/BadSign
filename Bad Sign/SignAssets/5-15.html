The Camel totem assumes ascetism, endurance, and the ability to be satisfied with what a person has. As a matter of fact, the Camel does not possess anything. He always carries everything he has with himself. Also, he knows how to distribute everything reasonably and harmoniously.

On the other hand, he is mistrustful and always expects the worst and may despise everyone around them. By the way, the more the Camel likes the person, the more he mocks this person. The Camel does not hide his attitudes towards people and always says what he thinks. He is aware of his advantages and tries to get what he needs relying on himself only. This sign is a rather reliable and hard-working person, who is hiding his extremely serious attitude towards life under the mask of laughter and flippancy. He is always ready to forgive much to his wife, relatives, and children, who are encouraged to be independent from the very beginning.
