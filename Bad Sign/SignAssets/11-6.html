Psoglav is a name of elder race of beings, human in form but with a head of a dog, with one big eye. Gentle in nature, but with gruesome appearance, they were driven out of all places above the ground.

Child born in this sing bear special sadness of ever longing for a place it never saw, and friend it never had. Sadness can make them wise and appreciate every friend like a treasure, even those not deserving.
