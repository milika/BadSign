Villa is a nature spirit in a form of long haired girl. She loves to sing and dance. Her hair is so precious that if it is torn she will die at the spot. She wears ever delicate shroud, without whom she looses her will to live.

Children born on special days can be blessed by a Villa. Boys grow strong and bold, girl beautiful and playful. They don't take life to seriously, and are usually better for it. Later love becomes their life, but if they are not careful it can turn to spite and vengeance.
