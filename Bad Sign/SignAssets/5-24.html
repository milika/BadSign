The year of the Donkey is the crisis completion and the beginning of abundance period. The Donkey is the symbol of peace, stability, patience, and big internal power.

A person of the Donkey totem is full of working efficiency, endurance, patience, peace, complaisance, calmness, and extreme modesty. He has the ability and desire to do many things with his hands. Such a person approaches any life situation being sensible and balanced. Having a firm character, he still needs an additional pulse necessary for him to start work. When the world is in conflicts and distemper, people of the Donkey sign become passive, asocial, and obstinate; they do not wish to do anything. It is considered a hopeful sign when a person, who was born in the Donkey year, has irregular prolonged features, slightly protruding strong teeth, and big ears.
