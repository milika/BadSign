The Lynx year usually brings trials, tests, and unexpected phenomena. We remove the scales from our eyes and see the true events, changes, and accidents. Sometimes, when people deserve it, the Nightingale totem may reveal.

People of the Lynx totem have something cattish in their appearance. Their behavior is unpredictable. They can be peaceful and calm now, and all of a sudden, they might be full of rage and hatred. The Lynx sign people don't seem to be organized, though they require order in everything from other people. The Lynx totem people often seem to be floppy, though they can psych up and get mobilized instantly. They are full of optimism and ideas. They have the talent of prophecy. People of the Nightingale totem have a beautiful silvery voice. They are strict enough, peaceful, and self-respecting.
