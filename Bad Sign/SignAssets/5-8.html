The Turtle is rather asocial, sluggish, and self-assured. People of this sign live their life, while having lots of toadeaters and adulators. They have a good memory and always come back to the same old things - as if beating the air. Such people always set goals for themselves and try to achieve them slowly but steadily. The supreme charisma of the year is wisdom and full harmony in correlations with the nature and knowledge of the world. It is considered a hopeful sign, if a person, born in this sign, has thick skin and some dark marks on his back.

The absence of the 'tough shell of a tortoise' should be seen as the sign anti-totem feature. People, who hold the anti-totem, are nervous and easily raised. They try to create an artificial "shell" around themselves and protect themselves with various masks, but they easily get depended on others.
