The Swan year is connected with spiritual unity of people. It is strictly prohibited to spoil water during this year. This year can bring miracles; on the other hand it can be the year of greed, deceit, backstreet intrigues, and airy promises.

People of this sign tend to be hard in terms of materialistic values. The Swan totem people are unselfish, proud, and faithful to supreme ideals. Also, they have an inclination for self-immersion and meditation. They do not pay attention to material things. They are in their own world, which is sometimes apart from the external world. Also, this sign is extremely affectionate towards its closest people and very faithful in love. The sign simply can't live without his close people. As a rule, the Swan totem person has a long graceful neck.
