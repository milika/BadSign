One of the daughters of elder deity Baba is Babaroga. She looks like an ugly old woman with single horn on its forehead, yet she wields unimaginable power. Protection of children is hears assignment.

Children that fall to Babaroga protection are lucky indeed. They live happy and beautiful childhood and are blessed with inner sight that transcends skin deep appearances. But as soon as they stop wanting to be children Babaroga leaves them and they are often left feeling lonely for the rest of their days.
