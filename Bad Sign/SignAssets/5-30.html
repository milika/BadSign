The Owl year is the one when it is necessary to pay attention to the dark side nature of things, as the darkness power is very strong in this year. The Owl totem people have their own schedule of life - they may sleep during the day and stay up in the nights.

They are very good clairvoyant, foretellers, and organizers of secret societies. These people are rather asocial, mystical, and hypochondriac (especially men). They live mysterious life and depart this life mysteriously as well. The best representatives of this sign strive for protection of the supreme values, though they not always can resist superior forces of the opponent. They can fully reveal themselves in a friendly environment only.
