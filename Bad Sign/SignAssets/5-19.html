In comparison to restive and egoistic Horse of Chinese horoscope, the Horse in Zoroastrian calendar is the symbol of endurance and justice. This year is connected with oaths, agreements, and penalty for injustice.

The Horse totem person is the master of his fate. He is fair and brave. He loves the nature and open space; he likes traveling and going in for sports. Being the guard of law and justice, he can crush down anyone who will try to break any formality. On the other hand, he can confront society, trying to achieve something. People of the Horse sign show their force in masses only. They are people of an athletic constitution with a proud bearing. They have big teeth, stiff and ungovernable hair, and big beautiful eyes.
