The Deer is the symbol of ascension and supreme power. People of this sign are gifted with the ability to see through evil and make it obvious for others. Also, they do their best to prevent evil.

The Deer totem person is full of impetuosity and pride. They understand that they are special, but they feel some loneliness and dissatisfaction with what have already been achieved. They desire to create something new and lead others for the better. The less this person doubts and looks back, the better it will be for him. It is desirable that such a person be flat-bellied and long-legged but not chatty.
