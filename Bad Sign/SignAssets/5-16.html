This is the year of good fortune, unpredictability, and freedom. This year can bring unexpected phenomena. The Hedgehog pierces the world of Darkness with his prickles. He either defeats the darkness or gives it a shake-up. The Hedgehog totem person reminds of boggards from folklore fairy tales, as he is unpredictable, spinous, and a little bit fidgety.

As a rule, he has a big head covered with tousled hair, sharp nose, and rather tiresome voice. People like him have a magnificent memory, especially visual memory. They tend to be rather scrupulous and like to pounce on every mistake or detail. They are rather faithful in friendship.
