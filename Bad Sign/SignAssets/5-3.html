He is an active person, who loves to struggle in extreme conditions and is not afraid of any dangers. People of this sign are men of honor or travelers, who can't stay in one place for a long time. Raman is the Wolf year protector. Raman is god of nature, which is connected with life circulation in the universe. He grants power of volcano and fire of supremacy to the person who deserves these. These gifts allow the person to enter the powerful rhythms of forces majeure and rise on the crest of a huge wave. It is the charisma of victory over the forces of nature and the opportunity to survive in extreme conditions. It is very good when a person born in this year has a strong, athletic figure and powerful neck.
