The year is under Apam-Napata's protection. He is god of heavenly, terrestrial, and underground rough streams of waters. The supreme charisma of the year is karma purification and the greatest secrets of world harmony understanding. This year reminds of the fact that everything falls back into its place - "the Snake biting its tail". The year is connected with karma requitals. Something that happened a long time ago comes back during this year.

The Snake totem gives a person variability, flexibility, mystical mind, and intuition. People of this totem tend to be honest and faithful. As a rule, something secret and latent is more important for them than something external and obvious. The Snake totem person is thin, flexible, nimble, and reticent. His eyes are deep in his head, and he has a strong look.
