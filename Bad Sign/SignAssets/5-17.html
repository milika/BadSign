The year is connected with harmony, beauty, searches of the supreme sense, and motherly bases revelation. The Fallow Deer year is the year of a person's conscience test. The Fallow Deer totem people are aristocratic in manners and rather graceful with a gorgeous figure and easy gait. They are easily recognized for their divinely charming and languishing eyes. They are rather romantic, artistic, capricious, and sensitive. As a rule, they brightly reveal their talents in art. On the other hand, they lack reason and common sense.

The Fallow Deer anti-totem reveals in roughness, excessive rationalism, and inactive way of living. Such people talk smut and curse. They have short thick legs and a fat face. Nevertheless, they like to dress in beautiful clothes, which emphasize their ugliness.
