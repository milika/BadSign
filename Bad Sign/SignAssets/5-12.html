In compassion to the Rooster of the Chinese horoscope, the Rooster of the Zoroastrian horoscope is a warrior, reformer, who never dives into the bushes. Very often, people of this sign have difficulties with finishing what they have started before and often do not get the things through. On the other hand, the best Roosters always manage to find a pearl grain.

People of this sign are rather knightly, active, and fearless. They do not think themselves without home and children. The Rooster totem person is always full of vigorous energy and plans. They love clothes (especially hats) and like to cut a dash. Also, they turn red often. People of this sign have rather mobile features and live mimicry. The Rooster sign people tend to be frank and tend to burn their bridges.
