The person, who was born in this sign, should constantly support the supreme creative basis in himself and people around him. He has to be adroit, quick, and ready to fight and destroy evil any moment. The supreme charisma of the sign is the Everlasting Life defeating the Death. The Mongoose is the symbol of constancy, fidelity, speed, and the ability to find the right way in any situation.

People of the Mongoose totem should be able to react quickly to any changes. They are to be unpredictable in their behavior but very frank. If a person born in the Mongoose year moves like in a dance and his hands and legs are thin and fast, then it is seen as a hopeful sign.
