Zmay is created from the eldest of animals, when an animal reach certain age and wisdom it sprouts fiery wing and becomes a Zmay. Their primary roll is protection and bringers of rain. Their weakness is for beautiful girls and when they fall in love they don't care about the rain anymore, and drought is brought.

Children born under the protection of a Zmay tend to be fierce and strong, life for the is an easy game, but get bored easily, and are prone to distractions. Those who learn to control their wild nature do great thing and are loved by all around them, especially opposite sex.
