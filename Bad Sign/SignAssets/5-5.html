The Spider is one of the absolute symbols. Its eight chelae symbolize the eight parts of the world, and its web serves as the symbol of the universe. The Spider year is the one of finding harmony and supreme sense.The Spider totem people are rather silent and modest. They like to establish various associations, which is a way of 'weaving their web'.

The Spider totem is connected with noesis and knowledge, which defeats the darkness and ignorance. People of this sign are slow, purposeful, grasping, and sensitive. As a rule, such people have long arms and prehensile fingers. They should be able to do a lot with their hands: sewing, knitting, spinning, darning, and mending, for instance. People of this sign act silently and modestly, staying it the background. They can silently stop any attempts of their enemies to unite against them. Very often, people of the peacock sign are informal leaders.
