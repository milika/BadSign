You love your personal freedom and want to explore different places, ideas, and people. You don't miss a single detail and tend to be a perfectionist. You are gifted with acute precision and patience - two qualities many signs struggle with. However, you keep a safe distance from others, so you may appear aloof and quite introverted. You lean toward technical fields like science, or mentally stimulating, philosophical studies.
