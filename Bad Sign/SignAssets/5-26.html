The years of Eagle are the years of construction, serious plans, and projects to be realized in life. It is the year of people unity.

Those who hold the Eagle totem have a proud bearing and beautiful, sort of, toreutic profile. Their voice is strong and adequate, and they know how to behave properly in any society. These people do not think themselves outside of their collective or clan, and they can sacrifice themselves to the big deed. People of the Eagle sign can predict the future. They are the statehood defenders, and they do not fear any obstacles or barriers. Many people do not love them for their aristocratism, high ideals, and knightly behavior. People of this sign tend to be rather ascetic in private life.
