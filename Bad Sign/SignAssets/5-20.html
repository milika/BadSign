The Cheetah runs fast; he instantly finds his bearings in spacious areas. This sign can adapt to life in extreme conditions. The Cheetah always aspires to foreseeing any danger and estimating all possible results and ways out. This sign likes to fight. Even if you see him recede in some cases, do not think that he gave up. He does it so that to accumulate his force and take in the rear. People of the Cheetahs sign are martial, aggressive, fearless, and artful with uncontrollable temper. They are good players and can change everything to suit their own needs.

The Cheetah totem person is a fair, noble defender. He is flat-bellied, cat-graceful, but very strong with an unfaltering gaze.The Cheetah anti-totem is a coward, weak, and miserable person, who is indiscriminate in making acquaintances.
