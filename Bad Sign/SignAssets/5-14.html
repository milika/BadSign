The Badger sign is connected with the memories of the past - storehouses, funds, banks, warehouses, and heavy cargos of memories. The sign is rather strong and steady. A person of this totem tends to be a very forehanded owner, who is economical, practical, efficient but reticent enough. He always has a set of ready decisions for any situation. One can never guess his occupation based on his appearance and actions. It is quite natural for this sign to keep its lifework in secret even from its closest people. People of this sign tend to be of the average height and solid build, inclined to fat. They have pinched features and big eyes. Badgers always keep their life memories and are very conservative. Their distinctive feature is their talents in logic, mathematics, research, and psychology.
