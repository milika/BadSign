The Raven year is the one of injustice, isolation, persecution, and epidemics. People of the Raven totem are serious, shrewd, and detached. They do not have the ambition of commanding and they do not like to obey. They can get rid of any trusteeship or oppression. They have the ability to foresee the events. The Raven's supreme charisma is the prophet charisma.

As a rule, people of this sign get married rather late or never have a family at all. Even in marriage, people of this sign remain lonely in their soul. The real Raven is very fastidious and neat. The Raven never lives on the efforts of others.
