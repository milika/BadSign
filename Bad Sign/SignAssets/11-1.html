Alla is the hailstorm demon that bring destruction to crops and the minds of men. It devours everything in its path, even trying to eat the sun and the moon...

Children born under the eye of an Alla are usually dark skinned and thin, even thought they have ferocious appetite. They are mild tempered and smart, but when weather turns bad they become loners. Great strength resides in them, if they can conquer their appetite for all.
