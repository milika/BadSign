You represent luck in beginning new things, and are very self-aware and concerned about your status in the world. You place a high value on life experiences, wanting to learn as much as possible from the triumphs and challenges they offer. You can appear a bit jaded and ruthless to others, but that is because you see things as they are and thus tend to be cynical. You dislike being judged, and if you feel under scrutiny your self-esteem takes a blow. You're this way because you actually are an expert (most of the time), and give solid advice. As serious as you are about life, you know how to find the humor in most situations, too.
