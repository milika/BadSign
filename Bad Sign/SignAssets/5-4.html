The Storks are wanderers, but they feel rather attached to their relatives and friends. Despite their craving for wandering, sooner or later they return to their original place eventually. As a rule, people of the Stork sign are silent singles who usually have only one love in their life. They are used to relying on themselves only. This sign tends to feel alien and melancholic in society. Society doesn't recognize them often.

The supreme charisma of this sign is the ability to see and know far beyond what other people do. Most often, this sign tends to be thin with prolonged features and long legs. They seem to be deep in thoughts.
