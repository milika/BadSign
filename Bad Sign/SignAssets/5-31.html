The Falcon Year is the time of fission and struggle for justice. People of the Falcon totem are high-flyers, preachers, and religion reformers. They tend to be brave, very proud, and full of patriotic ideas. They do a lot for the lost knowledge renewal.

Recklessness is the Falcon sign's characteristic feature. They might regret what they have done, but they can't help doing it over and over again. On the other hand, they can do a lot of good things being wisely managed. However, they will never stand dictatorship over them. Also, they are excellent disorganizes; when it is necessary to disorganize something, people of the Falcon sign will do it with pleasure. Many things in their life are exaggerated and artificial. Many people of this sign often think about death and what will remain after them.
