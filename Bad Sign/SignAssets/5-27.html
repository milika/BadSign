The Fox year makes trivialities important. Many important things depend on little things and chance. That is why this year is considered to be the one of lot.

People of the Fox sign tend to be sharp-witted and able to expose intrigues.People, which are born in this sign, have very mysterious, strange, and changeable destinies. As a rule, they never live a monotonous life. People of the Fox totem are adroit, humorous, and astute. However, they are a little bit coward and never head for trouble, but they are very provident and can get to the core of any intrigue and show the right way to others. They like to punish meanness and reveal dullness and conceit. People of the Fox sign tend to look like a fox. Hence, they are rather slim with chiseled features.
