This is the year of seduction, game, and horrible deceits. Evil hides its real face and deceives people these years. The Peacock totem person is cheerful and many-sided. He likes to play and you can never know what to expect from him. He aspires to reveal his creative potential and make all sides of his talent visible for others. Every day, he discloses his new features. He never stays still and always astonishes people around him. Moreover, he surprises himself sometimes.

Material things and property are not of the major importance for him. Very often, people do not like those who were born in the Peacock year, as they like to show off much. People of this sign tend to have many plans, some of which are completely crazy and impossible to realize.
