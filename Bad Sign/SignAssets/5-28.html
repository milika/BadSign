The year of Dolphin is the one of travels, big plans, and mysterious phenomena. Also, it is the year of help and rescue.

The Dolphin is the most rational sign. People, which were born in the year of Dolphin, are wise and inspired idealists, who provide help in difficult situations to those who need it. They can distinguish between the Light and Darkness. They feel faithful to the supreme ideals and have a bent to philosophy and peacemaking. They are rather mysterious in their behavior. Also, they always keep an eye on everything and are able to provide help, not demonstrating it. They take the hardest part of work. There is somewhat telepathic about them, as they are able to accept and transfer thoughts.
