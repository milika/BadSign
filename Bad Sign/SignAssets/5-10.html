The year of the Squirrel sign is the one of crimes against innocents; under the color of friendship evil brings bad things to life. The more people fuss and trot about - the worse it is for them and the more often they have to deal with the things they wanted to escape from - "a squirrel in a wheel". It is especially necessary to be wary of hexing during the year of Squirrel.

The person of the Squirrel totem is lively, clever, thrifty, quick, and very efficient. He always aspires to create a family. This sign has an unsteady character. It can fall into depressions and gloominess. However, the Squirrel is not afraid of death at all, as they perceive it as a transition to another world. The sign is rather conservative in its attachments, affections, and habits. The person possessing the Squirrel totem is rather nimble and slim with small features. His eyes are always in movement.
